Personal Development

Description

During the minor Visual Interface Design I have learned a lot about the design process. This page is dedicated to my personal development during the minor.

Excursion: Brussels

During this minor we went on an excursion to Brussel. We visited the Design Museum, the Atomium and did a bunch of sight seeing. During this trip the class really got to know eachother better and the city of Brussels inspired us as designers.

Minor conclusion

Looking back on it I'm happy to have chosen this minor for my third year of Communication and Multimedia Design. I learned a lot about the design process and I'm glad to have been able to apply this in my own projects. I also learned a lot about myself as a designer and have made new friends.

My biggest achievement from this minor is the fact that I discovered new software that managed to raise my design skill to the next level. It gives me the perfect opportunity to combine my passion for web and animation to create beautiful and interactive designs.
